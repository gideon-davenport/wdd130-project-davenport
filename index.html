About the Transformers TV Show

Transformers is a sci-fi action series where alien robots can transform into vehicles, weapons, and more. The ongoing battle between the heroic Autobots and the villainous Decepticons spans galaxies and threatens Earth.

This website is a class project for WDD 130 and is designed as a fan hub to highlight characters, episodes, and toys inspired by the show.

Main Characters

Optimus Prime

Leader of the Autobots. Wise, brave, and always willing to sacrifice himself to protect others.

Bumblebee

A loyal scout and warrior. Known for his courage, speed, and strong bond with humans.

Megatron

Ruthless leader of the Decepticons, obsessed with power and control over Cybertron and the universe.

Starscream

Decepticon air commander, always scheming to overthrow Megatron and take command.

Featured Episodes

  • Episode 1: Arrival on Earth – The Autobots crash-land on Earth and discover a new world full of allies and danger.
  • Episode 5: Battle for the AllSpark – Both sides race to find the AllSpark before it falls into the wrong hands.
  • Episode 10: Fall of Cybertron – Flashbacks reveal how the war began and why the Transformers left their home planet.
